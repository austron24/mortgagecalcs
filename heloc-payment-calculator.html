How HELOCs Work

A HELOC is a revolving line of credit secured by your home. It has two phases:

Draw Period (5-10 years)

  • Borrow up to your credit limit as needed
  • Usually only required to pay interest
  • Can repay and reborrow during this time

Repayment Period (10-20 years)

  • Cannot borrow additional funds
  • Must pay principal and interest
  • Payments are typically higher than draw period
