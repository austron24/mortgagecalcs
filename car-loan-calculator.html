Auto Loan Tips

Getting the best car loan can save you thousands over the life of the loan.

  • Check Your Credit: Better credit scores qualify for lower rates
  • Shop Around: Compare rates from banks, credit unions, and dealers
  • Shorter Terms: Pay more monthly but save on total interest
  • Large Down Payment: Reduces loan amount and monthly payments
  • Avoid Long Terms: 72-84 month loans cost more in interest
