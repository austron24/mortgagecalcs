Understanding Jumbo Loans

Jumbo loans are mortgages that exceed the conforming loan limits set by Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac. They're used for luxury homes and properties in high-cost areas.

2024 Conforming Loan Limits

  • Standard areas: $766,550
  • High-cost areas: Up to $1,149,825
  • Amounts above these limits require a jumbo loan

Jumbo Loan Requirements

  • Credit score: Typically 700+ (often 720+)
  • Down payment: Usually 10-20% minimum
  • DTI ratio: Generally under 43%
  • Cash reserves: 6-12 months required
  • Documentation: More extensive than conforming loans
