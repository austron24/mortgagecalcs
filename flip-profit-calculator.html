House Flipping Guide

Successful house flipping requires accurate cost estimation and understanding key investment metrics.

The 70% Rule

A quick formula for evaluating deals: Maximum Purchase Price = (ARV √ó 70%) - Repair Costs

This leaves room for profit and unexpected expenses. Experienced flippers may use 65-75% depending on market conditions.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Underestimating renovation costs - Always add 10-20% contingency
  • Ignoring holding costs - Delays kill profits
  • Overestimating ARV - Be conservative with comps
  • Forgetting selling costs - Commissions and closing add up fast
  • Not accounting for permits - Required work takes longer
