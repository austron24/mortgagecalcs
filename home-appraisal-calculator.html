How Appraisals Work

Professional appraisers use the sales comparison approach, analyzing recent sales of similar properties (comps) and adjusting for differences to estimate your home's market value.

Common Adjustments

  • Size: ~$50-150 per square foot difference
  • Bedrooms: ~$5,000-15,000 per bedroom
  • Bathrooms: ~$5,000-20,000 per bathroom
  • Garage: ~$10,000-25,000 per bay
  • Condition: Varies significantly

Appraisal Costs

Professional appraisals typically cost $300-$600 for single-family homes, more for larger or unique properties.
