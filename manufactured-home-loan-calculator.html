Manufactured Home Financing

Manufactured homes (built after 1976 to HUD standards) have different financing options than traditional site-built homes. The key factor is whether you own the land.

Real Property vs Chattel

  • Real Property: Home + owned land on permanent foundation. Qualifies for traditional mortgages with better rates.
  • Chattel: Home only (renting land). Considered personal property like a car. Higher rates, shorter terms.

Tips for Better Rates

Buy or own land, install on permanent foundation, choose double-wide or larger, and work with lenders experienced in manufactured housing.
