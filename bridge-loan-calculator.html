What is a Bridge Loan?

A bridge loan is short-term financing that helps homeowners buy a new home before selling their current one. It "bridges" the gap between the two transactions by providing funds for a down payment.

How Bridge Loans Work

  • Borrow against equity in your current home
  • Use funds for down payment on new home
  • Repay when current home sells
  • Typical terms: 6-12 months

Considerations

Pros: Buy before selling, avoid contingent offers, move on your schedule

Cons: Higher interest rates, fees add up, risk if home doesn't sell quickly
