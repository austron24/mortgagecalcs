How Construction Loans Work

Construction loans are short-term financing used to build a home. Unlike traditional mortgages, the lender releases funds in "draws" as construction progresses, and you only pay interest on the amount drawn.

Typical Draw Schedule

  • Draw 1 (Land/Foundation): 15-20% - Site prep, permits, foundation
  • Draw 2 (Framing): 20-25% - Framing, roofing, windows
  • Draw 3 (Mechanical): 15-20% - Plumbing, electrical, HVAC
  • Draw 4 (Interior): 20-25% - Drywall, flooring, cabinets
  • Draw 5 (Final): 15-20% - Finish work, landscaping, final inspection

Construction-to-Permanent Loans

Many borrowers use a construction-to-permanent loan, which converts to a standard mortgage once construction is complete, avoiding the need for two separate closings.
