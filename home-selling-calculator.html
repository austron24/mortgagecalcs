Seller Costs Explained

Sellers typically pay 8-10% of the sale price in total costs, with agent commissions being the largest expense.

Common Seller Expenses

  • Agent Commission: 5-6% (often split between buyer's and seller's agents)
  • Closing Costs: 1-3% (title insurance, transfer taxes, attorney fees)
  • Repairs: Varies (pre-listing fixes, inspection repairs, buyer credits)
  • Staging: $500-5,000 (furniture rental, professional staging)
  • Moving: $1,000-5,000 (depending on distance)
