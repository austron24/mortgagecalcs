Understanding USDA Loans

USDA loans are government-backed mortgages for rural homebuyers who meet income requirements. They offer 100% financing with competitive terms.

USDA Loan Requirements

  • Location: Property must be in USDA-eligible rural area
  • Income: Household income must be at or below 115% of area median
  • Credit: Typically 640+ score preferred
  • Primary residence: Must be your main home
  • U.S. citizen or permanent resident

USDA Guarantee Fees (2024)

  • Upfront fee: 1.0% of loan amount (can be financed)
  • Annual fee: 0.35% of remaining balance (paid monthly)
  • Much lower than FHA mortgage insurance
