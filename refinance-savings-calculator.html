When Does Refinancing Make Sense?

Refinancing can save you money, but it depends on several factors:

  • Rate Reduction: Generally, a 0.5-1% rate drop is worth considering
  • Time Horizon: You need to stay long enough to recoup closing costs
  • Closing Costs: Typically 2-5% of the loan amount
  • Loan Term: Shorter terms save interest but increase payments
  • Break-Even Point: Key metric - months until savings exceed costs
