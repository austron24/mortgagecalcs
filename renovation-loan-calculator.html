Understanding Renovation Loans

Renovation loans allow you to finance both the purchase and improvement of a home in a single mortgage, based on the after-renovation value (ARV).

How It Works

  • Get approved based on what the home will be worth after renovations
  • Renovation funds held in escrow and released as work completes
  • Contractors paid through draw schedule
  • Final inspection ensures work completed to standards

Eligible Improvements

Most renovation loans cover: structural repairs, kitchen/bath remodels, room additions, roofing, HVAC, energy efficiency upgrades, accessibility modifications, and more.
